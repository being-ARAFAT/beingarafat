About Me

The story so far

A student navigating creativity, leadership, and purpose — all at once.

Hi...I'm Arafat Rahman Rahi, a first-year college student who completed my SSC from Tejgaon Government High School, Dhaka, Bangladesh, in 2026. My journey in design began with curiosity and experimentation, from Canva to Photoshop and Illustrator, eventually evolving into a powerful way to express ideas and solve creative challenges.

As President of the TGHS Arts & Crafts Club, I discovered that creativity thrives in community. I directed the TGHS Trivium Festival 2025, an experience that sharpened my understanding of event management, team coordination, and creative leadership under pressure.

Through Red Crescent Youth (RCY) volunteer work, I've learned that service is the most meaningful form of design — shaping experiences that actually matter to people's lives.

Beyond academics and design, I have a deep passion for exploring the world, discovering new places, cultures, and perspectives. Gaming is another interest that has helped me develop critical thinking, strategic decision-making, and collaboration skills. Every experience—whether through creativity, leadership, volunteering, gaming, or travel—contributes to my personal growth and broadens my understanding of the world.
